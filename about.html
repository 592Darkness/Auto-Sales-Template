About AutoElite

Guyana's premier destination for quality vehicles and auto parts

Our Story

Founded in 2015, AutoElite has grown from a small family-owned business to become Guyana's leading automotive dealership and parts supplier. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide Guyanese drivers with access to premium vehicles and quality parts at fair prices.

For over a decade, we've been committed to exceptional customer service, transparent business practices, and building lasting relationships with our clients. Today, we're proud to offer an extensive inventory of new and pre-owned vehicles, as well as a comprehensive selection of OEM and aftermarket parts.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted automotive partner in Guyana, transforming the car buying and ownership experience through integrity, excellence, and innovation.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency in all our dealings, building trust with our customers one interaction at a time.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from the vehicles and parts we sell to the service we provide.

Community

We're proud to be a part of the Guyanese community and committed to giving back through various initiatives.

Sustainability

We're dedicated to promoting eco-friendly practices and offering fuel-efficient and electric vehicle options.
