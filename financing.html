Frequently Asked Questions

What credit score do I need to qualify for financing?

While we work with buyers across the credit spectrum, a score of 650+ typically qualifies for our best rates. However, we have financing options for all credit situations and work with multiple lenders to find the best solution for you.

How long does the financing approval process take?

Many applications receive an initial response within minutes. In some cases, additional verification may be required, which can take 24-48 hours. Our finance team works diligently to process all applications as quickly as possible.

Can I get pre-approved before I shop for a vehicle?

Yes! We recommend getting pre-approved before shopping. This helps you understand your budget and streamlines the purchase process. Pre-approvals are valid for 30 days and involve a soft credit inquiry that won't affect your credit score.

What documents do I need to apply for financing?

You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of income (pay stubs or tax returns), proof of residence, and insurance information. For specific situations, additional documentation may be required.
